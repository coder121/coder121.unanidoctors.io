About Syed Asaduddin Ahmed

Dr Syed Asaduddin Ahmed is world renowned Unani Doctor. He has completed his graduation in 1997 from Govt. Nizamia Unani Medical College, one of the renowned institution in Hyderabad India. He is the first Unani Doctor to receive officially licensed from Ministry of Health(M.O.H)to practice in United Arab Emirates since 2002. He has established Unani Medical Department in Al Amin Medical Center ,Sharjah,Dermah International Medical Center,Dubai and Hind Medical Centre Sharjah.
